Al-Kom

An Ode to Levantine Fire and Flavor

Nestled in the heart of the city, Al-Kom brings together generations-old Levantine recipes and modern culinary technique. Every plate is prepared with charcoal-grilled meats, hand-milled spices, and produce sourced from local family farms, served in a warm, candlelit dining room.

Our Story

Three Generations, One Fire

Al-Kom began in 1978 as a small charcoal grill run by Nabil Haddad in Amman, Jordan. His grandson, Chef Yousef Haddad, brought the family recipes abroad in 2011, refining them alongside classically trained chefs while keeping the wood-fired grill at the center of every dish. Today, Al-Kom seats sixty guests across two dining rooms and a private courtyard, and every recipe on the menu traces back to that original grill in Amman.

Our Mission

To honor Levantine hospitality by pairing time-tested recipes with attentive, modern service, so every guest leaves feeling like family.

What We Stand For
